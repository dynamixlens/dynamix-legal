Privacy Policy

Operated by Dynamix · Effective date: June 17, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Dynamix (“we”, “our”, “us”) accesses, uses, and protects data obtained through the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) and the Amazon Advertising API. Dynamix is operated by an individual Amazon seller and uses these APIs solely to manage and analyze its own Amazon selling and advertising activity.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the internal application built by Dynamix to integrate directly with Amazon's APIs. The application is for internal business use only and is not offered, distributed, or made available to third-party sellers, advertisers, agencies, or external clients.

2. Information We Access

Through Amazon's APIs, the application may access and process data associated with our own Amazon seller and advertiser account, including:

  • Seller profile and account information
  • Product listing, catalog, and inventory data
  • Order, fulfillment, and shipment data
  • Pricing, offer, and competitive information
  • Financial, settlement, and accounting reports
  • Brand analytics, search, and performance signals
  • Advertising profile information
  • Advertising campaign, ad group, keyword, targeting, and creative data
  • Advertising performance, reporting, and attribution data
  • Authorization tokens and credentials required to access Amazon APIs

3. How We Use the Information

Data obtained through the Amazon APIs is used exclusively for internal operations of Dynamix, including:

  • Monitoring orders, inventory, and fulfillment activity
  • Analyzing sales, pricing, and financial performance
  • Managing and optimizing advertising campaigns and budgets
  • Reporting, dashboards, and operational decision-making
  • Forecasting and inventory planning

We do not use this data to provide services to other sellers, advertisers, agencies, or any external parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data obtained from Amazon APIs with any third party. Access is limited to the individual operator of Dynamix. Where established third-party analytics tools are used to support internal reporting, data is processed only within those authorized services under their own terms and is never sold or redistributed.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data obtained through Amazon APIs, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage of API credentials and authorization tokens
  • Access restriction based on operational need
  • Multi-factor authentication on accounts that hold sensitive credentials
  • Periodic review of access permissions and stored credentials

In the event of a confirmed security incident affecting Amazon Information, we will follow our defined incident response procedures, including reporting the incident to security@amazon.com within 24 hours of confirmation.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as it is needed for the internal business operations described above, or as required to satisfy Amazon's API terms and applicable law. Data that is no longer needed is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights and Contact

This application processes data belonging to the operator's own Amazon seller and advertiser account. If you believe data about you has been processed by this application in error, or if you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

dynamixlens@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the application after a change indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
